Distance-Based CP

Dong et al.12 proposed a simple procedure for computing BSSE corrections of fragment-based methods:

  1. Compute the energy of each fragment. Let \(E_i\) be the energy of the \(i\)-th fragment.

  2. For each fragment create a new system which is the fragment plus any ghost atoms within some threshold (12 suggested 4 Angstroms). Let \(E_i'\) be the energy of the \(i\)-th fragment plus the included ghost atoms.

  3. Compute a BSSE correction to \(E_i\) as \(E_i'\) minus \(E_i\).

Note

Dong et al.12 Suggest only including 50% of the BSSE correction.
