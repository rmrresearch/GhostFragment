Systematic Molecular Fragmentation Capping MethodÔÉÅ

As part of the SMF method Deev and Collins6 introduced a capping method. Say we are breaking a bond \(X-Y\) and that \(X\) is in the fragment, then a hydrogen atom is placed at the point \(\mathbf{r}_H\) given by:

\[\mathbf{r}_H = \mathbf{r}_X + c\left(\mathbf{r}_Y - \mathbf{r}_X\right),\]

where \(\mathbf{r}_X\) is the position of \(X\), \(\mathbf{r}_Y\) is the position of \(Y\), and \(c\) is the ratio of a normal \(X-H\) bond to a normal \(X-Y\) bond.
