Electrostatic Field Adapted MFCC (EFA-MFCC)ÔÉÅ

In an effort to extend the DCLC and EC-MFCC methods to charged systems, Jiang et al.27 introduced the electrostatic field-adapted MFCC (EFA-MFCC) method. EFA-MFCC is DCLC with electrostatic embedding via EC-MFCC Charges (N.B. aside from details on how the fragments are formed, the manuscript is actually a bit sparse on the details of the EFA-MFCC method and this is our best guess as to how the EFA-MFCC method waas implemented).
